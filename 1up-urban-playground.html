Our Mission

The Urban Playground is a world-first experiential hub where community, sport and creativity collide. A place to play, create and belong.

Skateboarding is now an Olympic sport — yet safe, indoor spaces for young people to learn and grow are rare. We changed that. 1UP is that space.
