🔒 Privacy Notice
Effective date: May 2025
Your privacy matters to us. This notice explains what personal information we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect it — in compliance with the Protection of Personal Information Act, 4 of 2013 (POPIA).

1. Who We Are

1-Up Urban Playground is an entertainment venue operating in South Africa. We are the responsible party for personal information collected through this booking form.

2. What Information We Collect

When you submit a venue hire enquiry, we collect:

  • Full name
  • Phone / WhatsApp number
  • Email address
  • Company or organisation (if applicable)
  • Event details (type, date, attendance)

3. Why We Collect It

We use your information only to:

  • Process and respond to your venue hire enquiry
  • Contact you regarding availability and pricing
  • Generate a quote or venue hire agreement
  • Respond to queries or feedback

We will not use your information for unsolicited marketing or share it with third parties without your explicit consent.

4. How Long We Keep Your Information

  • Enquiry records: up to 12 months after your event date, then securely deleted.
  • Financial records: up to 5 years as required by South African tax regulations.

5. How We Protect Your Information

Your data is stored in a secured, access-controlled system. Access is restricted to authorised 1-Up Urban Playground staff only.

6. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third party, except where required to deliver our service or by law.

7. Your Rights Under POPIA

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct any inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Object to the processing of your personal information
  • Lodge a complaint with the Information Regulator of South Africa
